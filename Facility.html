Department Facilities

Explore the various resources and collaborations of the department

Laboratories

Our department houses modern laboratories tailored for hands-on learning and innovation in emerging technologies like Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cyber Security, and Cloud Computing. These labs are critical in fostering project-based learning and practical exploration.

  • Technology Centre: Promotes skill development, innovation, and entrepreneurship with advanced systems and incubation support.
  • Ideal Lab: A creative lab for prototyping and interdisciplinary innovation guided by mentors.

Library Resources

The departmental library serves as a vital academic hub, offering a curated collection of textbooks, journals, project reports, and question papers. Students benefit from national and international e-resources via institutional subscriptions.

Additionally, access to platforms like NPTEL and digital databases facilitates self-paced learning, research, and preparation for competitive exams.
