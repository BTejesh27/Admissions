About us

A Student-Driven Learning Ecosystem

Our department thrives on breaking traditional hierarchies, fostering an environment where seniors and juniors collaborate as equals. Together, we build a community focused on growth, innovation, and real-world impact.

Our faculty members serve as mentors rather than instructors, encouraging students to take ownership of their learning while providing crucial guidance when needed. They create opportunities for students to explore, experiment, and excel on their own terms.

  • Design thinking and industrial training form the core of our curriculum
  • Students lead events, projects, startups, and internship initiatives
  • Peer-to-peer mentoring replaces traditional senior-junior barriers
  • Collaborative learning environment with industry-aligned projects
  • Faculty members mentor without micromanaging, fostering independence

Key Focusing Areas

Design Thinking
Real-world Problem Solving

Students learn to apply design thinking methodologies to identify, analyze, and solve real-world challenges. This human-centered approach fosters innovation and practical solution development.

Software Development
Full-Stack & UI/UX Excellence

Our curriculum emphasizes both technical development skills and user experience design. Students master the full software development lifecycle while creating intuitive, accessible, and visually appealing interfaces.

Startup Ecosystem
From Concept to Company

We nurture an entrepreneurial mindset and provide resources for students to transform ideas into viable startups. Our program covers business model development, pitching, funding strategies, and product-market fit.

About SRKR

Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKREC), established in 1980, is a self-financed academic institution of coeducation striving to provide a high quality technical education to engineering aspirants. Being one of the premier and well-established technical institutions of the country, it continues to render service to the nation and the world at large with its alumni holding highly prestigious positions and making substantial contribution
